About us

Alex Tan - STEM Tutor

Alex Tan holds a PhD in Physics from the National University of Singapore (NUS) and brings over a decade of experience in private tuition. He is passionate about helping students achieve their full potential.

With patient and effective teaching methods, Alex simplifies complex topics, builds confidence, and fosters a love for learning. Under his mentorship, countless students have unlocked their academic success.

Ed - English Tutor

Ed, with a Master's in Finance from National University of Singapore (NUS), brings over five years of experience in teaching English at primary and secondary school levels. With 12 years of international education across the US, Europe, and Southeast Asia, he leverages cross-cultural expertise to help students excel in diverse education systems.

Specializing in Cambridge PET, AEIS, O-level english, and IELTS, Ed creates tailored lessons and fosters measurable progress. His dedication and effective methods have empowered numerous students, particularly Chinese learners, to succeed academically.
